Internship

You will learn to be a future leader when you work with us as an intern. We'll use your suggestions to influence the future and enhance the quality of life for our clients. Not always will it be simple. You'll enjoy every second of it, though, if you're up for the challenge. And we'll be there for you at every turn.

Sheltech Realtors Limited's internship program provides Nigeria's higher institutions students the chance to obtain job experience and the chance to collaborate with experts. The following group of applicants would find the knowledge and abilities acquired by the program's conclusion to be extremely helpful for their future careers:

  • SIWES (Student Industrial Work Experience Scheme) participants must complete the program (INDUSTRIAL TRAINEE).
  • College students who are looking to spend their holidays gaining useful work experience

Educational Qualifications & Functional Skills:

  • CGPA - 2.50 minimum
  • WAEC - Minimum 6 B’s & C’s
